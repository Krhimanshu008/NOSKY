For a CRM website, avoid describing only the pipeline. Most businesses don't buy a CRM because it has a pipeline—they buy it because it helps them increase revenue, improve customer relationships, and gain visibility over their sales process.

A good CRM website should answer three questions:

1. **What problems does it solve?**
2. **How does it solve them?**
3. **Why is it better than managing sales in Excel or WhatsApp?**
